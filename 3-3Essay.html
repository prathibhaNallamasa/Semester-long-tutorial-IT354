Usability issues to be aware of

By reading the article i came to know about the issues which I should keep in mind while working withe websites. The top 10 issues I found interesting are :

Do not use too small font sizes :

Long passages are harder to read and to read brief sentences readers need more time. It also holds for links, buttons, forms, search boxes and other elements.

Not use unclear link text

Using link texts which are unclear can cause confusion for the users.

Having dead links:

Having a dead link leads to a blank page or page not found error. this makes the user a bit confused and we do not want any one to land on a blank page or a dead end.

Having many animations :

Having too many animations in a webpage can cause a trouble for the user to focus on the text or the information.

Banner Blindness:

Web users ignore everything that looks like an advertisement to them. eventhough an add is noticed, it is still ignored. Since users have constructed web related schemata for different tasks on the Web, when searching for specific information on a website, they focus only on the parts of the page where they would assume the relevant information could be, i.e. small text and hyperlinks. Large colourful or animated banners and other graphics are in this case ignored.

Physical Consistency

This means website needs to have a consistent design through out the structure. the fonts and color schemes must be consistent and should not be totally different from your base scheme.

Readability

Readability describes the degree to which the meaning of text is understandable, based on the complexity of sentences and the difficulty of vocabulary. Indexes for readability usually rank usability by the age or grade level required for someone to be able to readily understand a reading passage. Readability is not legibility.
